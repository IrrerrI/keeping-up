About Us

TOTIM is a photojournalist-driven narrative reporting platform.

We equitably license work from the archives of prominent and up-and-coming photojournalists to produce deeply historic and timeless photo-stories that inspire empathy, solidarity and civic engagement.

At the heart of TOTIM's mission is the desire to expose and strengthen the connective tissue that binds us as a human community.

    Introduction

    Totim respects your privacy and is committed to a user experience that protects your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our data practices, emphasizing our commitment to not collecting or storing any private user data. By using the Totim app, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy.

    1. Data Collection Practices

    Totim does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its users. The app is designed to operate without the need for personal data collection, ensuring complete privacy and anonymity in usage.

    2. Usage and Device Data

    Totim does not collect or retain any usage data, device data, or identifiers. Any in-app experiences or preferences are stored only locally on your device and are not accessible to us.

    3. Third-Party Sharing

    Totim does not engage in any third-party data sharing, sales, or transfers. No personal or usage information is collected, and thus no information is shared with external entities.

    4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    Totim does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies that collect personal or usage data. Your activity within the app is private and is not monitored or recorded by us.
